Rio de Janeiro Real Time Bus Tracker

This web app allows you to view the real-time locations of cityhall buses in Rio de Janeiro. You can also filter by a specified bus line. The red markers indicate that there are many buses on the region, while the yellow and the green markers indicate that there is a normal and a small amount of buses on the region respectively. A single bus is indicated by a blue bus icon, and when clicked on, show information such as the bus line, the speed and the bus identifier. The live bus data is fetched from the Rio de Janeiro's cityhall API (data.rio).
